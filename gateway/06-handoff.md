# Phase 6: Handoff

Final 1-2 weeks. Goal: cleanly transition ownership to the client and close the engagement.

## Objectives

1. Confirm the client can operate independently
2. Document outcomes and lessons learned
3. Identify expansion opportunities
4. Close cleanly

## Activities

### Operational Validation
- Client team runs the system independently for 1-2 weeks with FDE on standby
- Monitor for questions, issues, and gaps in documentation
- Fill gaps in real time — every question reveals a missing doc or training gap

### Outcomes Documentation
- Final KPI report comparing baseline to current state
- ROI calculation if applicable
- Executive summary for the sponsor to share upward

### Expansion Planning
- Identify adjacent use cases surfaced during the engagement
- Prioritize by impact and feasibility
- Leave a roadmap, not a sales pitch

### Engagement Closure
- Retrospective with the client team (use templates/retro.md)
- Internal retrospective — what would you do differently?
- Update `memory/` with lessons learned
- Archive engagement artifacts

## Anti-Patterns

- Irish goodbye — always close formally, even if the relationship continues
- Leaving loose ends — every open item should have an owner
- Overselling the next engagement during handoff — focus on what was delivered

## Outputs

- Final outcomes report
- Expansion roadmap (if applicable)
- Client retrospective notes
- Internal retrospective and lessons learned
- Updated memory/ for future reference

## Done.
