# Phase 1: Kickoff

The first 48 hours of an engagement. Goal: establish credibility, align on scope, and set the operating rhythm.

## Day 1 Checklist

- [ ] Access granted: email, Slack/Teams, VPN, relevant systems
- [ ] Introductions with key stakeholders (see skills/stakeholder-mapping.md)
- [ ] Review existing documentation the client has shared
- [ ] Load the relevant `domains/` folder — read overview, roles, vocabulary first
- [ ] Identify your executive sponsor and day-to-day point of contact
- [ ] Confirm the stated problem/opportunity and who defined it

## Day 2 Checklist

- [ ] Draft initial engagement plan (scope, milestones, cadence)
- [ ] Schedule recurring check-ins (weekly with sponsor, daily with POC)
- [ ] Begin environment access requests (see extensions/)
- [ ] Start `memory/` notes for this engagement
- [ ] Identify quick win — something you can demo or deliver in week 1

## Anti-Patterns

- Don't disappear into the codebase for a week. Stay visible.
- Don't agree to scope expansion in the first meeting. Listen, then respond async.
- Don't assume the stated problem is the real problem. Validate in discovery.

## Outputs

- Engagement plan (use templates/sow.md as starting point)
- Stakeholder map (use skills/stakeholder-mapping.md)
- Initial environment assessment (use scripts/env-scanner/)
